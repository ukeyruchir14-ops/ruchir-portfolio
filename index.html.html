I don't just edit videos — I build the story that keeps viewers watching.

With 5+ years of professional editing experience, I've worked across long-form YouTube content, social media reels, cinematic wedding films, and AI-powered design workflows.

Currently a Video Editor at Finnovationz Pvt. Ltd., I handle everything from rough cut to final delivery — audio design, motion graphics, color grading, and thumbnails that push CTR.

I've built a reputation for fast turnaround without compromising quality, leveraging AI tools to streamline transcription, subtitles, and repurposing. My work is always client-first: your vision, refined.

Before Finnovationz, I freelanced for 3+ years, producing cinematic wedding highlights and social reels for photographers and studios across India. That period gave me something no single job could — the ability to handle the full production pipeline solo, under pressure, every time.

I collaborate closely with creative teams, content marketers, and founders to ensure every frame serves a purpose. No filler. No generic cuts. Just content that earns attention.
