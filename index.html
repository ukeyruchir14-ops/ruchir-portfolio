I didn't start with a studio job. I started solo — shooting, cutting, and delivering wedding films and social content for clients across India. 4+ years of freelancing taught me speed, adaptability, and how to deliver quality when no one is watching over your shoulder.

That foundation landed me at FinnovationZ Pvt. Ltd. — editing for FinnovationZ by Prasad — one of India's most followed finance channels with 2.64M+ subscribers — where I now edit long-form YouTube content, Shorts, and design thumbnails that move the needle on CTR.
